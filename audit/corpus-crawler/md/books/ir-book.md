# Introduction to Information Retrieval

This is the companion website for the following book.

[Christopher D. Manning](http://nlp.stanford.edu/%7Emanning/), [Prabhakar Raghavan](https://research.google/people/prabhakarraghavan/) and [Hinrich Schütze](http://www.cis.uni-muenchen.de/personen/professoren/schuetze/), *Introduction to Information Retrieval*, Cambridge University Press. 2008.

You can order this book at [CUP](http://www.cambridge.org/us/catalogue/catalogue.asp?isbn=9780521865715), at your local bookstore or on the internet. The best search term to use is the ISBN: [0521865719](http://search.yahoo.com/search?p=0521865719).

The book aims to provide a modern approach to information retrieval from a computer science perspective. It is based on a course we have been teaching in various forms at [Stanford University](http://www.stanford.edu/class/cs276/), the University of Stuttgart and the [University of Munich](http://cislmu.org).

We'd be pleased to get feedback about how this book works out as a textbook, what is missing, or covered in too much detail, or what is simply wrong. Please send any feedback or comments to: informationretrieval (at) yahoogroups (dot) com
